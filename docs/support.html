Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to servers or the cloud.

How do I delete an entry or letter?

Swipe left on any letter in the inbox, or tap "Delete" at the bottom when reading a letter.

Why aren't my notifications working?

Can I edit a journal entry after saving?

Yes! Tap the same date again and your previous entry will load. Make changes and save.

What happens to scheduled letters if I delete the app?

All data is deleted permanently when you delete the app. There's no way to recover it.

Will my data sync across devices?

No. MindMail is local-only. Each device has its own separate data.

Can I export my journal?

Currently not supported. Future updates may add this feature.

Is there a character limit?

Journal prompts: 140 characters each
Letter subject: 50 characters
Letter body: 500 characters

How many letters can I schedule?

Maximum 100 scheduled letters at a time (for performance).

Troubleshooting

Notifications not appearing

  • Check notification permissions in Settings
  • Ensure "Do Not Disturb" is off
  • Open the app - letters appear in inbox even without notification

App feels slow

  • Restart the app
  • If you have 90+ scheduled letters, delete some old ones

Lost my data

  • Data is local only - if you deleted the app, data is gone
  • Check if you have iCloud backup enabled (restoring may recover it)
